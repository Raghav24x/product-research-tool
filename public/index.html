How this tool works & privacy ▼

What powers it: This tool uses the Google Gemini API (2.5 Flash) with Google Search grounding. When you click Evaluate, your inputs are sent to Gemini, which researches the tool in real time and produces a structured evaluation.

What we store: Nothing. Evaluations are not saved on our servers. Your inputs are not logged, tracked, or used to train any AI model. Each evaluation is a one-time request.

Where data goes: Your inputs are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. Read Google's API terms.
