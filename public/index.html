How this tool works & privacy ▼

What powers it: This tool uses the Anthropic Claude API (Sonnet 4.6) with web search enabled. When you click Evaluate, your inputs are sent to Claude, which researches the tool in real time and produces a structured evaluation.

What we store: Nothing. Evaluations are not saved on our servers. Your inputs are not logged, tracked, or used to train any AI model. Each evaluation is a one-time request.

Where data goes: Your inputs are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's data handling policy applies. Read Anthropic's privacy policy.

Who built it: This tool is built and maintained by Cash & Cache. Questions or feedback? Reply to any newsletter email or drop a comment on the article.
